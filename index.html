Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the emotion recognition?

BabyMood achieves 80-90% accuracy for the five core emotions (happy, sad, angry, surprised, neutral) in good lighting conditions with a clear view of the face.

What age range does BabyMood work for?

BabyMood is designed for babies and toddlers from 6 months to 4 years old. The accuracy improves as facial features become more defined with age.

Is my child's data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, delete photos after analysis, and never share your data with third parties. We comply with all relevant children's privacy regulations.

Does BabyMood work offline?

BabyMood requires an internet connection for the emotion analysis as it uses our secure cloud AI. However, you can view your history and previous results offline.

Can BabyMood detect health issues?

BabyMood is not a medical device and cannot diagnose health conditions. It's designed to help parents understand emotional states, not to replace professional medical advice.

Which platforms will BabyMood be available on?

BabyMood will be available on both iOS and Android devices at launch.
