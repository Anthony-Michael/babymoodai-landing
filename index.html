Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the emotion recognition?

Our technology achieves 85-90% accuracy for the five basic emotions (happy, sad, angry, surprised, neutral) in babies and toddlers aged 6 months to 3 years.

Is my child's data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, and all photos are processed securely and deleted after analysis unless you specifically choose to save them.

What age range works best with the app?

BabyMood AI works best with children aged 6 months to 3 years, when facial expressions are developing and becoming more distinct.

Do I need to take photos constantly?

Not at all. You can take photos whenever you want insights into your child's emotional state. The app is designed to be used as needed.

Will this work if my child is crying?

Yes, our technology can distinguish between different types of crying and the emotions behind them.

Is there a subscription fee?

BabyMood AI will offer both free and premium tiers. The basic emotion recognition will be available in the free version, with advanced features in the premium tier.
