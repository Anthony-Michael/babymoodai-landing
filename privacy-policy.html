Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2025

At BabyMood AI, we take your privacy and the protection of your child's data extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your and your child's information when you use our mobile application and services. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Important Note: BabyMood AI is designed for parents and legal guardians to use on behalf of their children. Our service is subject to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and we are committed to complying with these regulations.

1. COLLECTION OF YOUR INFORMATION

We may collect information about you and your child in various ways. The information we may collect via the Application includes:

1.1 Personal Data

When you register for our Application, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your child's age range (not exact birthdate)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)

1.2 Facial Images and Emotion Data

Our core functionality involves:

  • Processing photos of your child to analyze facial expressions
  • Generating emotion analysis results
  • Storing historical emotion data (if you enable this feature)

2. PARENTAL CONSENT

In compliance with COPPA and GDPR, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). As a parent or legal guardian:

  • You must complete our parental consent process during registration
  • You have the right to review your child's information
  • You can request deletion of your child's data at any time
  • You can revoke your consent at any time

3. USE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Application
  • Process and analyze facial images to detect emotions
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Application
  • Understand and analyze how you use our Application
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • Communicate with you about updates or changes to our Application
  • Find and prevent fraud

4. DISCLOSURE OF YOUR INFORMATION

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

4.1 By Law or to Protect Rights

If we believe the release of information is necessary to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers to: perform tasks on our behalf and to assist us in providing the Application. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and can only use it for specified purposes.

4.3 What We Do NOT Do With Your Information

We do NOT:

  • Sell or rent your child's personal information or facial images to third parties
  • Use facial images or emotion data for advertising purposes
  • Share your child's data with third parties for their marketing purposes
  • Create profiles of children for commercial purposes unrelated to our service

5. DATA RETENTION AND SECURITY

5.1 Data Retention

We implement strict data retention policies:

  • Facial images are processed in real-time and deleted within 30 days
  • Emotion analysis results are stored for 90 days by default
  • You can manually delete data at any time through the app
  • Account information is retained until you request deletion

5.2 Security Measures

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with strict access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Employee training on data protection

However, please be aware that no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CONTROL

You have certain rights regarding your and your child's personal information:

  • Access: You can request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your data
  • Restriction: You can request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@babymoodai.com.

7. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits users of our Application that are California residents to request certain information regarding our disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please send an email to privacy@babymoodai.com.

8. POLICY FOR CHILDREN

We are committed to protecting children's privacy. Our Application is designed for use by parents and legal guardians on behalf of their children. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children without proper parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

9. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. CONTACT US

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at:

Email: privacy@babymoodai.com
